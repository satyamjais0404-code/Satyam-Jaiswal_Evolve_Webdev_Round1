About Vidyut

Vidyut is a dynamic technical fest organized byEvolve-the Interdisciplinary Technical Club of MANIT Bhopal,aimed at igniting innovation,creativity,and hands-on engineering among students.

Fest serves as a platform for showcasing cutting-edge ideas through technical competions,workshops,project exhibitions,and innovation challenges across diverse domains such as engineering,technology,and applied science

Viduyt'25 Events

EV-Expo

An arena of new-age electric vehicles witnessing stalls and booths displaying 25+ electric vehicles in the luxury and long- range segments. Test rides and on-spot displays will be available to take a feel of the drive, comfort, and technological innovation, and to spread awareness and promote green mobility for electric vehicles. Connect with EV manufacturers, industry experts, and enthusiasts to explore the future of e-mobility. Discover government policies, charging solutions, and upcoming innovations shaping the EV landscape. Join us in driving the change towards a cleaner, smarter, and more sustainable tomorrow.

Tech Track 2.0

Tech-track 2.0 provides a dynamic opportunity for all the innovators, critical thinkers and solution orientation minds, providing everyone a stage to challenge their analytical and technical skills to solve Industry-relevant problems, starting from the problem statement to the final pitch. This is an incredible chance to gain hands-on industrial exposure and compete against the brilliant minds, pushing your problem solving skill to a next level!

Industry Talk

Engaging sessions featuring industry experts and startup founders from the EV domain, sharing real-world insights, innovative journeys, and future trends in electric mobility. A unique opportunity to learn directly from leaders shaping India's EV landscape. These interactive discussions will infrastructure challenges, and cutting-edge technological advancements. Participants will gain valuable perspectives on entrepreneurship, innovation strategies, and the evolving market dynamics that are driving the future of green mobility in India and beyond.

Research Symposium

Poster Presentation Competition provides students, research-enthusiasts, and professionals with a dynamic platform to exhibit their technical knowledge, research, and innovative ideas in the field of electric vehicles and emerging technologies. The participants will display their research-related projects, problem statements, and analysis, where they will be judged by industry experts. This event aims to foster collaboration between academia and industry by highlighting real-world solutions and forward-thinking concepts. Prizes will be awarded to the impactful entries by industrial leaders.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.
Who can participate in Vidyut?
Students from colleges and universities across India can participate. Some events may also allow school students or individual participants.
How can I register for events?
You can register through the official Vidyut website by visiting the Events section and completing the registration form.
Is there any registration fee?
ALL events are free .
What are the main events in Vidyut 25?
Vidyut'25 includes hackathons, robotics competitions, coding contests, tech talks, EV expos and much more.
Why should I attend Vidyut 25?
Vidyut offers a platform to learn, compete, network, and showcase your skills while experiencing one of the most electrifying college fests of the year.
